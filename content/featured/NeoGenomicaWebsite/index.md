When I arrive in the company, the website was dark, AI generated and doesn't have a integrated ecommerce. Now, the Website has a responsive version with many landing pages for main exams, such as focusing in long life exams, hereditary cancer and others. Now is possible the user buy a genetic test by the ecommerce integrated into the website.

I interviewed employees, c-level, doctors and give some users informations from Clarity. Benchmarks and competitor analysis was done frequently. I prototyped all screens at Figma. I mentored a designer. I comunicated the company needs to the programmer and managed the activities in Scrum Sprints of a week. For the front-end, I worked with HTML5, Tailwind CSS and Alpine JS in Django templates. I worked with AI agents both for the design and for the front-end.
