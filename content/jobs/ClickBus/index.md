- During my time at ClickBus, I led the complete product design cycle (from discovery to delivery) for the products B2C and B2B I managed (ClickOferta, Whitelabel, Checkout), mentoring other designers and focusing on the experience of millions of passengers in a highly dynamic environment. Using intelligence based on data-driven research and user-centricity, I was responsible for transforming complex business challenges into clean interface solutions, basing on data, resulting in optimized Checkout conversion metrics (+7%) through A/B testing, reduced WhiteLabel cart abandonment (churn) (-11%) with user communication strategies on the platform, and improvements to the main high-season top-of-funnel through usability testing, problem identification, and solution validation with users, representing 2% of GMV during the evaluated period.
- I applied desk research, user testing, AB tests, user interview, CSD Matrix, Imapct Effort Matrix; I used Figma, Figjam, Miro, VWO and Amplitude; and I workerd with a multidisciplinar team with Product Managers, Tech Lead and stakeholders from other areas such as commercial, growth marketing and consumer experience.
